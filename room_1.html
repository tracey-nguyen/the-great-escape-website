The Book of Magic

You and a group of wizards learn about the book of magic and the power it holds. The tale of the book goes that a famous wizard named Kane Flinders hid the book as many dark wizards were trying to get a hold it for themselves. The book is now needed because a dangerous wizard is trying to destroy the wizarding world and the book of magic is our only hope to defeating this wizard.

Your headmaster recognises your talents and puts a team together to find the lost book of magic. You will need to use your magical knowledge and puzzle solving skills to find the book. Good luck.
