ABOUT US

OUR STORY

The Great Escape was found by two brothers Henry and William in 2014. Growing up, Henry and William loved playing puzzles and solving challenges, so much that they even created their own murder mystery games. When they found out about escape rooms, they knew that they had to share what their creations to the world and decided to open one up right in Broadway where it is still there today.

WHO CAN PLAY?

At The Great Escape, anyone aged 16 and over can play our escape rooms. We recommend you to play along with your friends and families, or with your work team as a team building exercise.

HOW TO PLAY?

In each escape room, there are different time limits in which you have to complete and solve the puzzles and challenges to escape. All players must work together and communicate so that you can help each other.
